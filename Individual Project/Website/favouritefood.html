My Favorite Foods

Nasi Lemak Ayam

A beloved Malaysian dish consisting of fragrant rice cooked in coconut milk, served with crispy fried chicken, sambal (spicy chili paste), boiled eggs, peanuts, and anchovies. It is a perfect balance of spicy, savoury, and sweet flavors.

Mee Kari Nyonya

A rich and creamy noodle soup with a Nyonya twist, featuring yellow noodles, coconut milk-based curry broth, prawns, tofu, and boiled eggs. The dish is aromatic and deeply satisfying.

Mee Kicap Ipoh

A simple yet flavorful Ipoh specialty, Mee Kicap combines springy noodles tossed in dark soy sauce and sesame oil, often accompanied by juicy beef or chicken. It's comfort food at its best.

Smoked BBQ Chicken

Chicken that is slow-cooked and smoked to perfection, with a smoky, juicy interior and a caramelized BBQ glaze. It is the ultimate barbecue treat, bursting with flavor.

Steak

Perfectly cooked steak, whether it's medium-rare or well-done, is a classic dish loved for its tender texture and robust flavor. Often served with sides like mashed potatoes or grilled vegetables, it is a hearty meal.
