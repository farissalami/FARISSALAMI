My Experience and Education

Professional Experience

Grab Rider (2 years)

During my studies, I worked as a Grab rider for two years, managing deliveries and customer service while balancing academic commitments. This experience taught me time management, customer interaction, and the importance of efficiency. It also gave me valuable exposure to the gig economy and the flexibility of working while studying.

Internship at IJN - CPR Faculty (Data Management Staff)

During my internship at the prestigious National Heart Institute (Institut Jantung Negara, IJN), I was part of the CPR Faculty team as a data management staff member. My responsibilities included managing patient data, assisting in research, and ensuring that all information was accurate and accessible. This experience honed my organizational skills and gave me insight into the healthcare industry, particularly in emergency medical care.

F&B Industry Experience (2 years)

Before pursuing my studies at UITM, I worked in the Food & Beverage (F&B) industry for two years. I started my career at Starbucks, where I learned the importance of customer service, teamwork, and maintaining high standards in a fast-paced environment. I also worked at The Rack Cafe, a Western restaurant in Selayang, where I gained experience in restaurant operations, food preparation, and customer service. This experience solidified my ability to work under pressure and reinforced my passion for the hospitality industry.

Bodybuilding and Martial Arts Trainer

As a passionate fitness enthusiast, I became a trainer in both bodybuilding and martial arts. I guided clients in strength training, fitness regimes, and proper techniques. This experience deepened my understanding of physical conditioning and sports sciences, and I learned how to motivate and inspire others to reach their fitness goals. It also helped me develop leadership skills, as I managed small groups and individual training sessions.

Education Experience

UPSR (12 years old - 4As)

At the age of 12, I completed the UPSR (Ujian Pencapaian Sekolah Rendah) exam with 4As, demonstrating my early academic potential and ability to excel in my studies from a young age.

PT3 (2018 - 5As)

In 2018, I achieved 5As in the PT3 exam, further establishing my academic capabilities. This achievement was significant as it prepared me for the next stage of my education, showing my continued focus and discipline in my studies.

SPM (2020 - 4As)

In 2020, I completed my SPM (Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia) exam with 4As, a crucial milestone in my educational journey. Despite the challenges posed by the COVID pandemic, I remained dedicated to my studies and achieved these results through hard work and perseverance.

Learning During the COVID Pandemic

The COVID-19 pandemic created unique challenges for students worldwide, and I was no exception. During this period, I adapted to online learning, developed new time management skills, and embraced the digital tools necessary for education. This experience taught me resilience and flexibility, proving my ability to adapt to changing circumstances and continue my education despite the disruptions caused by the pandemic.
